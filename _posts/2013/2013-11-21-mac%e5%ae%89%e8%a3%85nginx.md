1，http://nginx.org/en/download.html下载http://nginx.org/download/nginx-1.2.0.tar.gz

2,tar -xaf nginx-1.2.0.tar.gz

3,进入解压目录  chmod a+rwx *

4,./configure --without-http_rewrite_module

5,make && make install

6,sudo /usr/local/nginix/sbin/nginx
